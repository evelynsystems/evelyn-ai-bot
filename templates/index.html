AI Picker
Let the bot research and pick stocks automatically using news and price trends
trading mode
AI mode
ON = bot picks stocks automatically | OFF = you pick manually
AI mode is ON. The bot will ignore your manual stock list and pick its own stocks each cycle using news sentiment and price trend analysis. Your manual list is saved and will return when you switch back.
Manual mode is ON. The bot uses the stocks you picked in the Stocks tab. Switch to AI mode to let the bot pick automatically.
ai picker settings
The AI scans 50 popular stocks every cycle. It scores each one using news sentiment and 5-day/20-day price momentum, then picks the top performers. You get an email with the picks and reasoning every time it runs.
